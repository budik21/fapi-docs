:::info[no-header]
Defines specific, measurable statistical metrics used to quantify and track a player's performance in a single match. These metrics are primarily player-centric and can be secondarily aggregated across a season, team, or the player's entire career.
:::

---

## Enum Values

| Value | Description |
|---|---|
| `ASSISTS_GOAL` | A player contributes to a goal by making the final pass or play that directly leads to a teammate scoring the goal |
| `ASSISTS_GOAL_SECOND` | A player contributes to a goal by making the pass or play that sets up the teammate who provides the final pass or play directly leading to a goal |
| `ASSISTS_SHOT` | The total number of times a player successfully passes the ball to a teammate who then takes a shot on goal, regardless of the shot's outcome. This includes all intentional passes leading directly to a shooting opportunity, reflecting the player's contribution to creating scoring chances |
| `ASSISTS_SHOT_OFF_TARGET` | A player makes a pass or play that directly results in a teammate taking a shot off the target (shot wide of or over the goal, or hits the goalposrt or crossbar) |
| `ASSISTS_SHOT_ON_TARGET` | A player makes a pass or play that directly results in a teammate taking a shot on target and forcing the goalkeeper to make a save to prevent a opponent to score a goal |
| `ASSISTS_SHOT_OPEN_PLAY` | The total number of passes from open play that directly create a shot attempt by a teammate. |
| `ASSISTS_SHOT_SET_PIECE` | The total number of passes from set pieces that directly create a shot attempt by a teammate. |
| `BIG_CHANCES_CREATED` | The total number of clear scoring opportunities set up by a player for his teammates. |
| `BIG_CHANCES_MISSED` | The total number of clear scoring opportunities that were not converted into goals. |
| `BIG_CHANCES_SAVED` | The total number of clear scoring opportunities (big chances) that a goalkeeper successfully prevented from becoming goals through saves. |
| `BIG_CHANCES_SCORED` | The total number of clear scoring opportunities that were successfully converted into goals. |
| `BIG_CHANCES_TOTAL` | The total count of clear scoring opportunities, regardless of whether they were converted into goals or not. These occur in situations where a player can reasonably be expected to score, typically in a one-on-one scenario or from very close range, where the ball has a clear path to the goal, and the shooter faces low to moderate defensive pressure. Penalties are always classified as big chances. |
| `BALL_RECOVERIES` | The total number of times a player regains possession for their team during open play following a switch of possession, typically by collecting a loose ball, intercepting a pass, or picking up a deflection |
| `BLOCKED_SHOTS` | The total number of shot attempts by opponents that a player has blocked before they could reach the goal. This is a defensive metric |
| `CARDS_RED` | A player is sent-off the play, he receives a disciplinary penalty (red card) from the referee for serious misconduct, violent conduct, denying an obvious goal-scoring opportunity, or accumulating two cautionary penalties (yellow cards) in the same match |
| `CARDS_YELLOW` | The count of times a player receives a cautionary penalty (yellow card) from the referee for unsporting behavior, repeated infringements, delaying the restart of play, or other minor offenses. |
| `CARDS_YELLOW_SECOND` | The count of times a player receives a second yellow card in the same match, which results in a red card and sending off. |
| `CARRIES_PROGRESSIVE` | The total number of times a player receives the ball and carries (drives) it forward towards the opposition's goal before making a pass, taking a shot, or losing possession. A carry must result in the ball being moved at least 5 meters closer to the opposition's goal to be counted as progressive. |
| `CARRIES_TOTAL` | The total number of times where a player receives the ball and carries (drives) it in any direction before making a pass, taking a shot, or losing possession. The difference from Dribbles is that this metric focuses on the movement with the ball without actively attempting to take on and go past an opponent. |
| `CHALLENGE_LOST` | The total number of defensive challenges or contests where a player fails to win possession or prevent the opponent from advancing. |
| `CLEAN_SHEETS` | A player (not only a goalkeeper) achieves a clean sheet by preventing the opposing team from scoring any goals throughout the match. A player will be awarded a clean sheet only if he plays the full 90 minutes in a match |
| `CLAIM_HIGH_EFFICIENCY` | The percentage of successful high claims made by a goalkeeper in relation to the total number of high claim attempts. A higher value indicates greater effectiveness in dealing with aerial deliveries. |
| `CLAIM_HIGH_SAVED` | The total number of times a goalkeeper successfully catches or takes control of a high ball delivered into the penalty area, preventing the opponent from retaining possession. |
| `CLAIM_HIGH_TOTAL` | The total number of times a goalkeeper attempts to catch or take control of a high ball delivered into the penalty area, typically from crosses, corners, or set pieces, regardless of whether the attempt is successful. |
| `CLEARANCES` | A player kicks the ball away from the defensive area to avoid danger, often moving it upfield or out of play |
| `CLEARANCES_OFF_LINE` | The number of times a player clears or blocks a shot from crossing the goal line, preventing a certain goal. |
| `CROSSES_ACCURACY` | The percentage of successful crosses (passes from wide areas aimed at a teammate in front of the goal) completed out of the total number of cross attempts. Successful crosses are those received by a teammate and include crosses from corner kicks and set pieces. |
| `CROSSES_ACCURATE` | Total number of crosses pass - a pass from the side aimed at a teammate in front of the goal. The ball should move from the wide area towards the center near the goal which were successfuly completed. Includes the crosses from the corner kick and set pieces |
| `CROSSES_TOTAL` | Total number of crosses pass - a pass from the side aimed at a teammate in front of the goal. The ball should move from the wide area towards the center near the goal. Includes the crosses from the corner kick and set pieces |
| `DRIBBLES_EFFICIENCY` | The percentage of successful dribbles in relation to the total number of dribble attempts. A higher efficiency indicates a player's effectiveness in retaining possession after attempting to dribble past an opponent. |
| `DRIBBLES_LOST` | The total number of times a player attempts to dribble past an opponent but fails to do so, either by losing possession of the ball, being tackled by the opponent, or pushing the ball too far to retain control. |
| `DRIBBLES_TOTAL` | The total number of times a player try to dribble past an opponent |
| `DRIBBLES_WON` | The total number of times a player successfully dribbles past an opponent and retains possession of the ball. If the player pushes the ball too far and loses possession, the action is not counted as a successful dribble but is instead classified as an overrun. |
| `DUELS_AERIAL_EFFICIENCY` | The percentage of aerial duels won by a player in relation to the total number of aerial duels contested. A higher efficiency indicates greater effectiveness in winning aerial challenges. |
| `DUELS_AERIAL_LOST` | The total number of times a player fails to win the ball during an aerial duel against an opponent. |
| `DUELS_AERIAL_TOTAL` | The total number of times a player contests an aerial duel to win the ball, regardless of the outcome. |
| `DUELS_AERIAL_WON` | The total number of times a player successfully wins the ball during an aerial duel against an opponent. |
| `DUELS_EFFICIENCY` | The percentage of one-on-one duels successfully won by a player in relation to the total number of duels contested, whether on the ground or in the air. A higher efficiency indicates greater effectiveness in winning individual battles. |
| `DUELS_GROUND_EFFICIENCY` | The percentage of one-on-one contests on the ground successfully won by a player in relation to the total number of ground duels contested. A higher efficiency indicates greater effectiveness in winning ground challenges. |
| `DUELS_GROUND_LOST` | The total number of one-to-one contests on the ground where a player is unsuccessful against an opponent |
| `DUELS_GROUND_TOTAL` | The total number of one-to-one contests between a player and an opponent on the ground during. For every duel won, there is a corresponding duel lost, depending on the outcome. |
| `DUELS_GROUND_WON` | The total number of one-to-one contests on the ground that a player successfully wins against an opponent |
| `DUELS_LOST` | The total number of times a player is unsuccessful in a one-on-one duel with an opponent, whether on the ground or in the air |
| `DUELS_TOTAL` | The total number of times a player engages in a one-on-one duel with an opponent, whether on the ground or in the air. For every duel won, there is a corresponding duel lost, depending on the outcome. |
| `DUELS_WON` | The total number of times a player successfully wins a one-on-one duel with an opponent, whether on the ground or in the air. |
| `ENTRIES_FINAL_THIRD_TOTAL` | The total number of passes initiated outside of the final third of the pitch that reach the final (attacking) third, from the perspective of the team making the pass |
| `ENTRIES_PENALTY_BOX_TOTAL` | The total number of passes initiated from outside the opponent's penalty area that move the ball into the penalty area, from the perspective of the team making the pass |
| `ERRORS_LEAD_TO_GOAL` | A player's mistake usually during the open play allows the opposing team to score a goal |
| `ERRORS_LEAD_TO_SHOT` | A player's mistake usually during the open play allows the opposing team to take a shot no matter if the shot was on or off the target |
| `EXPECTED_ASSISTS` | Expected Assist (xA) represents the likelihood that a pass will lead to an assist for a goal. It evaluates the quality of a pass based on factors such as its placement, trajectory, and the context of the attacking play, as well as the probability that the resulting shot will be converted into a goal. |
| `EXPECTED_GOALS` | Expected Goals (xG) represents how likely a specific shot is to result in a goal. The value is calculated based on various factors such as the shooter’s position, distance from the goal, shot angle, and other pre-shot attributes. |
| `EXPECTED_GOALS_ON_TARGET` | Expected Goals On Target (xGOT) reflects the quality of a shot after it has been taken, considering placement of the shot to evaluate its effectiveness. From the goalkeeper's perspective, it represents how difficult it is to prevent the shot from resulting in a goal. |
| `EXPECTED_GOALS_ON_TARGET_FACED` | xGOT faced reflects (from the goalkeeper's perpective) how difficult it is to prevent shots on target from resulting in goals. It considers the xG of given shot and also taking into account placement of the shot to evaluate their likelihood of ending in a goal. |
| `EXPECTED_GOALS_NO_PENALTIES` | Expected Goals (xG) excluding penalty kicks, representing the likelihood of shots resulting in goals based on various factors such as position, distance, and angle, but without considering penalties. |
| `EXPECTED_GOALS_FACED` | The total xG value of all shots faced by a goalkeeper, representing the cumulative goal-scoring threat posed by opponents. |
| `EXPECTED_GOALS_NO_PENALTIES_FACED` | The total xG value of all shots faced by a goalkeeper excluding penalties, representing the cumulative goal-scoring threat from open play and set pieces. |
| `EXPECTED_GOALS_ON_TARGET_NO_PENALTIES_FACED` | The total xGOT value of all shots on target faced by a goalkeeper excluding penalties, reflecting the quality and placement of shots the goalkeeper must deal with. |
| `EXPECTED_GOALS_ON_TARGET_NO_PENALTIES` | Expected Goals On Target (xGOT) excluding penalty kicks, reflecting the quality of shots after execution based on placement and other factors, without considering penalties. |
| `FINISHING_QUALITY` | Finishing quality measures how effectively a player converts a given chance into a goal threat. Each chance is assigned a quality value based on the expected goals (xG) metric. However, not all shots on target pose the same level of threat to the goalkeeper. The expected goals on target (xGOT) account for the shot's placement and execution, reflecting its true difficulty for the goalkeeper. The greater the difference between xGOT and xG, the higher the quality of the player's finishing. Conversely, if the shot generates less threat than the original chance quality, it indicates a poorly executed finish. |
| `FINISHING_QUALITY_NO_PENALTIES` | Finishing quality excluding penalties, measuring how effectively a player converts chances into goal threats from open play and set pieces. |
| `FOULS_COMMITTED` | A player breaks the rules, usually through illegal contact with an opponent (a foul) or by playing the ball with their hand. |
| `FOULS_SUFFERED` | A player is fouled by an opponent, usually through illegal contact |
| `GOALS` | A player scores by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal and changing the match score, no matter if it was from open play, a penalty kick, a direct free kick, or any other situation. |
| `GOALS_BOX_IN` | A player scores by shooting the ball into the opposing team's goal from inside the penalty box, changing the match score. |
| `GOALS_BOX_OUT` | A player scores by shooting the ball into the opposing team's goal from outside the penalty box, changing the match score. |
| `GOALS_CONCEDED` | The total number of goals a team allows the opposing team to score during a match, including both goals scored by the opposing team and own goals |
| `GOALS_CONCEDED_PENALTY` | The total number of goals a team concedes from penalty kicks during a match. |
| `GOALS_FREE_KICK` | A player scores directly from a free kick and changing the match score |
| `GOALS_HEAD` | A player scores by heading the ball into the opposing team's goal,changing the match score. |
| `GOALS_NO_PENALTIES` | The total number of goals scored by a player excluding penalty kicks, reflecting their scoring ability from open play and set pieces. |
| `GOALS_OPEN_PLAY` | A player scores by shooting the ball into the opposing team's goal during the regular flow of the game, changing the match score. |
| `GOALS_OWN` | A player accidentally scores by getting the ball into their own team's goal, usually due to a mistake during open play, changing the match score and adding a goal to the opponent's team. |
| `GOALS_PENALTY` | A player scores by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal directly from the penalty kick |
| `GOALS_PREVENTED` | The difference between xGOT faced and actual goals conceded (excluding own goals), reflecting the goalkeeper's effectiveness in stopping high-quality shots on target. A negative value indicates the goalkeeper is maybe conceding easy goals, while a positive value shows they are preventing goals from high-quality shots. |
| `GOALS_PREVENTED_NO_PENALTIES` | The difference between xGOT faced (excluding penalties) and actual goals conceded (excluding own goals and penalties), reflecting the goalkeeper's effectiveness in stopping high-quality shots from open play and set pieces. |
| `GOALS_SET_PIECE` | A player scores by shooting the ball into the opposing team's goal from a set-piece situation (such as a penalty, free kick, or corner), changing the match score. |
| `GOALS_SET_PIECE_DIRECT` | A player scores by shooting the ball into the opposing team's goal directly from a set-piece situation (such as a penalty, free kick, or corner), changing the match score. |
| `GOALS_WINNING` | The player scoring the winning goal is the one who scores the goal that ultimately gives their team the lead that they do not relinquish. For example, in a 3-0 victory, it would be the goal that made the score 1-0. In a 5-3 victory, it would be the fourth goal scored by the winning team. |
| `GOAL_KICKS_ACCURACY` | The percentage of successful goal kicks, where the goalkeeper restarts play and the ball reaches a teammate, out of the total number of goal kicks taken. |
| `GOAL_KICKS_ACCURATE` | The total number of times the goalkeeper restarts play with a goal kick that successfully reaches a teammate |
| `GOAL_KICKS_TOTAL` | The total number of times the goalkeeper restarts play with a goal kick, regardless of whether the ball successfully reaches a teammate or not. |
| `HANDBALLS` | The total number of times a player illegally handles the ball with their hand or arm, resulting in a free kick or penalty. |
| `HIT_WOODWORK` | Number of times the player hit the woodwork (post or crossbar) |
| `INTOBOX_CORNERS_ACCURACY` | The percentage of accurate corner kicks into the opponent's penalty area and successfully find a teammate, including both direct deliveries and long passes from a corner. |
| `INTOBOX_CORNERS_ACCURATE` | The total number of corner kicks that successfully find a teammate in the opponent's penalty area, including both direct deliveries and long passes from a corner. |
| `INTOBOX_CORNERS_TOTAL` | The total number of corner kicks that reach the opponent's penalty area, regardless of whether they successfully find a teammate, including both direct deliveries and long passes from a corner. |
| `INTOBOX_FREEKICKS_ACCURACY` | The percentage of accurate free kicks into the opponent's penalty area in relation to the total number of free kicks attempted. |
| `INTOBOX_FREEKICKS_ACCURATE` | The total number of balls played directly into the opponent's penalty area from a free kick that successfully find a teammate. |
| `INTOBOX_FREEKICKS_TOTAL` | The total number of balls played directly into the opponent's penalty area from a free kick, regardless of whether they successfully find a teammate. |
| `INTERCEPTIONS` | A defending player stops a pass between opposing players anywhere on the pitch |
| `INTERCEPTIONS_BOX_OWN` | A defending player stops a pass between opposing players in the penalty box of their team |
| `KEEPER_SWEEPER_ACCURACY` | The percentage of successful sweeper actions completed by the goalkeeper out of the total number of sweeper action attempts, reflecting their effectiveness in winning the ball and retaining possession. |
| `KEEPER_SWEEPER_ACCURATE` | The total number of successful sweeper actions where the goalkeeper wins the ball and maintains possession for their team. |
| `KEEPER_SWEEPER_TOTAL` | The total number of actions performed by the goalkeeper to intercept passes, clear the ball, or prevent attacking plays, demonstrating proactive involvement in defensive situations regardless of whether the team retained possession of the ball afterward |
| `KEEPER_THROWS_ACCURACY` | The percentage of successful actions where the goalkeeper distributes the ball by throwing it to a teammate, out of the total number of such attempts |
| `KEEPER_THROWS_ACCURATE` | The total number of times the goalkeeper successfully distributes the ball by throwing it to a teammate, ensuring possession is maintained. |

| `LAYOFFS_ACCURATE` | The total number of backward passes by a player received with their back to goal that successfully reach a teammate, enabling the teammate to progress the attack. |

| `LONG_BALLS_ACCURACY` | The percentage of successful long balls (32 meters or longer) completed out of the total number of long ball attempts. This excludes throw-ins, crosses, and goalkeeper throws. Successful long balls are those received by a teammate. |
| `LONG_BALLS_ACCURATE` | The total number of passes, excluding throw-ins, crosses, and goalkeeper throws, that were 32 meters or longer and successfully completed (the pass was received by a teammate). |
| `LONG_BALLS_TOTAL` | The total number of passes , excluding throw-ins, crosses, and goalkeeper throws, that were 32 meters or longer. |
| `MATCH_LINEUP_FINISHING` | Indicates if the player was on the pitch at the moment the match ended, regardless of how many minutes he played in the match. |
| `MATCH_LINEUP_STARTING` | The player was selected as part of the team's starting lineup and began the match on the field, regardless of how many minutes he played in the match. |
| `MATCH_DRAWING_TEAM` | Indicates whether the player played for the team that drew the match (1 if yes, 0 if no) |

| `MATCH_MINUTES_PLAYED_LIVE` | The total number of minutes a player actively spends on the field as part of the team, calculated live. If he plays the full match without any extra time, 90 minutes are recorded. Added time is not included, but any extra time is counted. |
| `MATCH_MINUTES_PLAYED_LIVE_ONPITCH` | The total number of minutes a player actively spends on the field as part of the team, calculated live. Added time is included. |
| `MATCH_MINUTES_PLAYED_LIVE_INPLAY` | The total number of minutes a player actively spends on the field as part of the team, calculated live. Added time is included. We extract significant stoppages in play (e.g., injuries, VAR reviews) to provide a more accurate representation of actual playing time. |
| `MATCH_ON_PITCH_LIVE` | Indicates whether a player is on the pitch during the live phase of the match. |
| `MATCH_WINNING_TEAM` | Indicates whether the player played for the team that won the match (1 if yes, 0 if no) |
| `OFFSIDES` | The number of times a player is penalized for being in an offside position when the ball is passed to them by a teammate, resulting in a free-kick being awarded to the opposing team. |
| `GOALS_OWN_CONCEDED` | The total number of goals a goalkeeper concedes from own goals. |
| `PASSES_ACCURACY` | The percentage of successful passes completed out of the total number of passes attempted. Successful passes are those received by a teammate. This metric excludes throw-ins, goalkeeper throws, and crosses. |

| `PASSES_BACKWARD_TOTAL` | The total number of backward passes made by a player that move the ball towards their own goal. |
| `PASSES_FINAL_THIRD_ACCURACY` | The percentage of successful passes completed into the final (attacking) third out of the total number of passes attempted into this area. Successful passes are those received by a teammate and initiated from anywhere on the pitch |
| `PASSES_FINAL_THIRD_ACCURATE` | The total number of successful passes completed (the pass was received by a teammate) initiated from anywhere on the pitch that ends in the final (attacking) third, from the perspective of the team making the pass |
| `PASSES_FINAL_THIRD_TOTAL` | The total number of passes initiated from anywhere on the pitch that ends in the final (attacking) third, from the perspective of the team making the pass |
| `PASSES_FORWARD_TOTAL` | The total number of forward passes made by a player that move the ball towards the opponent's goal. |
| `PASSES_OPEN_PLAY_ACCURATE` | The total number of accurate passes completed by a player during open play, excluding set pieces. |
| `PASSES_OPEN_PLAY_ACCURACY` | The percentage of accurate passes completed by a player during open play in relation to the total number of open play passes attempted. |
| `PASSES_OPEN_PLAY_TOTAL` | The total number of passes attempted by a player during open play, excluding set pieces. |
| `PASSES_THROUGH_ACCURACY` | The percentage of successful through-ball passes that accurately penetrate the opposition's defensive line to set up a teammate one-on-one with the opposing goalkeeper, out of the total number of through-ball attempts. This excludes throw-ins, goalkeeper throws, and crosses. |
| `PASSES_THROUGH_ACCURATE` | The total number of successful through-ball passes that accurately penetrate the opposition's defensive line to set up a teammate one-on-one with the opposing goalkeeper. No throw-ins, keeper throws, and crosses are counted. |
| `PASSES_THROUGH_TOTAL` | The total number of attempts at through-balls, defined as passes that penetrate the opposition's defensive line to set up a teammate one-on-one with the opposing goalkeeper. No throw-ins, keeper throws, and crosses are counted. |
| `PASSES_TOTAL` | The total number of pass attempts made regardless of their success. The throws-in, goalkeeper throws and crosses are not included |
| `PASSES_FORWARD_TENDENCY` | The ratio or percentage of forward passes made by a player compared to all passes, indicating their playing style and tendency to advance the ball. |
| `POINTS_PLUS_MINUS` | Plus/minus statistic - positive value when the player's team scores a goal while the player is on the field, negative value when the player's team concedes a goal while the player is on the field |
| `PENALTIES_COMMITTED` | The number of fouls (or handballs) a player commits inside their own penalty area, resulting in a penalty kick being awarded to the opposing team |
| `PENALTIES_FACED` | The number of times a goalkeeper faced a penalty kick during regular play time. The penalties faced during the shootout are not included |

| `PENALTIES_WON` | The count of times a player is fouled in the opponent's penalty area, resulting in a penalty kick being awarded to their team |

| `PENALTIES_MISSED_SAVE` | The number of penalty kicks that were saved by the goalkeeper and did not result in a goal. |
