:::info[no-header]
PlayerPosition represents the general playing position category of a football player. This is a high-level classification that describes the player's typical role on the field.
:::

---

## Enum Values

| Value | Description |
|---|---|
| `GOALKEEPER` | The goalkeeper, the only player allowed to handle the ball within their own penalty area. |
| `DEFENDER` | A defensive player who primarily focuses on preventing the opposing team from scoring. |
| `MIDFIELDER` | A player who operates in the middle of the field, balancing defensive and attacking responsibilities. |
| `ATTACKER` | An attacking player who primarily focuses on creating scoring opportunities and scoring goals. |
