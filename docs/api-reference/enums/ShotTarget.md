## Enum Values

| Value | Description |
|---|---|
| `UNSPECIFIED` | Represents a NULL or UNKNOWN value. |
| `GOAL_LOW_LEFT` | A shot placed low into the bottom-left corner of the goal. |
| `GOAL_LOW_CENTER` | A shot placed low and centrally into the goal. |
| `GOAL_LOW_RIGHT` | A shot placed low into the bottom-right corner of the goal. |
| `GOAL_HIGH_LEFT` | A shot placed high into the top-left corner of the goal. |
| `GOAL_HIGH_CENTER` | A shot placed high and centrally into the goal. |
| `GOAL_HIGH_RIGHT` | A shot placed high into the top-right corner of the goal. |
| `WIDE_CLOSE_LEFT` | A shot that narrowly misses the goal to the left. |
| `WIDE_LEFT` | A shot that clearly misses the goal to the left. |
| `WIDE_CLOSE_RIGHT` | A shot that narrowly misses the goal to the right. |
| `WIDE_RIGHT` | A shot that clearly misses the goal to the right. |
| `WIDE_CLOSE_HIGH` | A shot that narrowly goes over the crossbar. |
| `WIDE_HIGH` | A shot that clearly goes high over the crossbar. |
| `WIDE_CLOSE_HIGH_LEFT` | A shot that narrowly misses high and to the left. |
| `WIDE_CLOSE_HIGH_RIGHT` | A shot that narrowly misses high and to the right. |
| `WIDE_HIGH_LEFT` | A shot that goes high and wide to the left. |
| `WIDE_HIGH_RIGHT` | A shot that goes high and wide to the right. |
